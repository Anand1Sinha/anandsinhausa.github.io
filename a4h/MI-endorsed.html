A4H Michigan — 2026 Endorsed Candidates

Michigan Primary Election · Tuesday, August 4, 2026

U.S. House · Michigan's 13th District
Shri Thanedar
Entrepreneur · Scientist · Congressman

An immigrant, entrepreneur, and scientist, Shri Thanedar has built a remarkable career through hard work and determination. In Congress he has remained engaged with Michigan's diverse communities while advocating for economic opportunity, innovation, and expanded access to education and healthcare.

He built the room where our community finally has a seat at the table. Founder of the Hindu, Buddhist, Sikh, and Jain American Congressional Caucus — a bipartisan group of more than two dozen lawmakers organized to combat religious discrimination and promote religious freedom for our communities in Congress.
He put anti-Hindu bigotry into the congressional record — permanently. Author of H.Res. 1131, the first congressional resolution condemning Hinduphobia and attacks on Hindu places of worship.
He is one of the very few Hindu American voices in Congress. Representation is functional, not symbolic — when legislation touching caste, religious symbols, or hate crimes comes before Congress, having a Hindu American in the room changes what gets caught before it becomes law.
He has delivered for his district, not just his community. On the House Small Business and Agriculture committees, Rep. Thanedar has worked to expand access to capital for small businesses and bring federal investment to communities in Michigan's 13th District.
U.S. House · Michigan's 11th District · Independent
Dr. Anil Kumar
Urologist · 30 Years Serving Oakland County

Dr. Anil Kumar is a respected Oakland County urologist who has spent more than three decades caring for Michigan families. Throughout his career he has earned a reputation for integrity, compassion, and public service.

Three decades of caring for Michigan families. A board-certified urologist with a long, trusted record of service to Oakland County patients.
An independent voice, free of partisan interests. Dr. Kumar brings an independent voice focused on affordable healthcare, responsible use of technology, economic opportunity, and accountable government.
A builder of community bridges. Founder of the MLK-Gandhi Institute for Non-Violence, working to strengthen communities through dialogue and service.
Principled leadership over partisan interest. A4H supports Dr. Kumar for his professional excellence and commitment to serving all constituents — not partisan interests.
Michigan State Senate · District 7
Dr. Rakesh Ramakrishnan
Physician · Community Leader

Dr. Rakesh Ramakrishnan is a physician and community leader dedicated to improving healthcare, education, and the quality of life for Michigan families.

A physician who understands what families are up against. Combines medical expertise with a deep commitment to improving healthcare, education, and economic opportunity for Michigan families.
A record of civic engagement, not just a campaign. Dr. Ramakrishnan has demonstrated a commitment to public service through both his medical career and civic engagement.
Practical solutions for working families. He understands the challenges facing working families and advocates practical solutions that strengthen communities while expanding opportunity.
Michigan House of Representatives · District 56 · Unopposed
Ashok Baddi
Entrepreneur · Community Leader

Ashok Baddi is a successful entrepreneur, community volunteer, and long-time advocate for economic growth, public safety, and educational excellence. He has been actively engaged with the Hindu American community for many years and understands the importance of civic participation.

Small business experience that translates into policy. A successful entrepreneur who understands what Michigan families and small businesses need to thrive.
Years of hands-on engagement with our community. A long-standing advocate for economic growth, public safety, and educational excellence.
Limited government, real opportunity. A4H supports Ashok for his commitment to limited government, economic opportunity, community engagement, and policies that help Michigan families and small businesses thrive.
