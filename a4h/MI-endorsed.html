U.S. House · Michigan's 13th District
Shri Thanedar
Entrepreneur · Scientist · Congressman

A4H is proud to support Shri Thanedar for Congress in Michigan's 13th District. An entrepreneur, scientist, philanthropist, and immigrant success story, Shri represents resilience, opportunity, innovation, and civic participation — values that resonate deeply with Hindu American families.

Founder of the Hindu, Buddhist, Sikh, and Jain American Congressional Caucus. Rep. Thanedar founded this bipartisan caucus in Congress, giving our community a formal, ongoing seat at the table on issues like temple security, religious discrimination, and civil rights.
Author of H.Res. 1131. The first congressional resolution condemning Hinduphobia and attacks on Hindu places of worship — putting anti-Hindu bigotry into the official congressional record.
Representation & Civic Participation. As one of the few Hindu American voices serving in Congress, Shri's leadership encourages broader civic engagement and political participation within Hindu American communities.
Commitment to Public Service. Shri has demonstrated long-standing engagement in philanthropy, community initiatives, and public service while advocating for economic development, healthcare access, and opportunity for working families.

Vote by Mail
Apply by August 1 · Ballot must arrive by August 4, 8pm

July 25 – August 2

Tuesday, August 4 · Polls open 7am – 8pm
Vote at your assigned polling place. Bring ID or sign an affidavit.
Find your polling place at mvic.sos.state.mi.us →
