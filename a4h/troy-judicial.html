Jessica Ann Hallmark
Attorney — judicial family background, Oakland County community ties

Jessica Ann Hallmark comes from a family with a judicial legacy and has built community ties across Oakland County. Detailed public information on her specific legal practice areas was not readily available through public sources at the time this guide was prepared.

Voters are encouraged to review her Oakland County Bar Association candidate questionnaire at ocba.org/judicial_candidates and her Oakland County Times candidate interview for a fuller picture of her qualifications.
