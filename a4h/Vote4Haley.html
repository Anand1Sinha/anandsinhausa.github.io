Why Haley Stevens

A4H endorses Haley Stevens for U.S. Senate. She is the only candidate in this race with a documented record of standing with Hindu Americans.

She condemned Hinduphobia in Congress — permanently on the record. H.Res. 1131 — bipartisan resolution condemning temple attacks and anti-Hindu bigotry across the United States.
She stood with Hindus facing persecution abroad. H.Res. 1085 — condemned the killing and persecution of Hindus and Sikhs in Afghanistan, backed their resettlement as refugees in the U.S.
She spoke out on Pulwama — in her first term, without being asked. February 2019 — formally condemned the Pulwama terrorist attack. Most American politicians still won't say what she said then.
She leads the only caucus in Congress on Dharmic community rights. Vice Chair of the Sikh Congressional Caucus — covering temple security, religious liberty, and civil rights for Hindu, Sikh, Jain, and Buddhist Americans.
She fights for the Michigan jobs our community depends on. Directed the Digital Manufacturing and Design Innovation Institute. Authored legislation to protect Michigan's manufacturing base.
