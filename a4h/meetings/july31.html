AMERICANS4HINDUS COMMUNITY GATHERING

Leadership Meeting with Dr. Japra

A focused gathering of community leaders, supporters, trustees, members, donors, and endorsed candidates to strengthen Indian American civic engagement and representation.

Date Friday, July 31
Time 12 PM to 3 PM
Venue Flavors • Farmington Hills

Why This Meeting Matters

Indian Americans are successful in many fields, but our civic voice needs stronger organization, better coordination, and deeper engagement with public leaders.

This meeting will bring like-minded community members together around a practical goal: build a stronger, more visible, and more effective Hindu American civic presence.

RSVP / Confirm Attendance

Who Should Attend

  • Community leaders and professionals
  • Potential trustees, members, and donors
  • Supporters of Hindu American civic engagement
  • Friends who want stronger representation

Meeting Goals

  • Introduce the mission and work of Americans4Hindus.
  • Build a local circle of trustees, donors, and members.
  • Connect endorsed candidates with the community.
  • Create a stronger volunteer and outreach network.
  • Leave with clear next steps and commitments.
