Special Focus — Building Indian American Civic Awareness
Purpose of the Gathering

A focused lunch meeting to discuss civic engagement, representation, and how our community can get involved.

Indian Americans are successful in many fields, but our civic voice needs stronger awareness, better coordination, and deeper engagement with public leaders.

This meeting will bring like-minded community members together to understand the issues, discuss practical ideas, offer suggestions, and explore ways to build a stronger and more effective Indian American civic presence.

The first goal is awareness and participation — helping more people understand why civic engagement matters and how each of us can contribute.

What We Hope To Achieve

  • Increase awareness of A4H and its civic mission
  • Discuss why Indian American representation matters
  • Hear suggestions and questions from community members
  • Identify practical ways people can stay involved

Who Should Attend

  • Community leaders and professionals
  • Friends interested in public affairs and representation
  • Supporters of Indian American civic engagement
  • Anyone who wants to understand and get involved
