About This App

What is the Family Directory?

The Balrampur Family Directory is a private members-only website built by Anand Sinha for the descendants of Raghuraj Prasad and Chandra Kala Devi. It is a living record of our family — who we are, where we live, and how we are connected to each other across three generations and multiple cities around the world.

It lives at anandsinhausa.com/balrampur and is accessible only to family members who have been personally invited and registered with a private family code. There are no advertisements. No subscriptions. No third-party access to your information. It is entirely built, hosted, and maintained by Anand.

Built for Privacy

How your information is protected

This app was designed with privacy as the primary concern — not an afterthought. Here is exactly how your data is kept secure.

🔐 Login required
Nothing is visible without a valid email and password. A stranger who finds the URL sees only a login screen — not a single family member's name.
🔑 Invitation only
Registration requires a private family code shared by Anand directly on WhatsApp. The code is never embedded in the website's source code or shared publicly.
🛡️ Encrypted passwords
Your password is stored using SHA-256 cryptographic hashing with a unique salt. It cannot be read, reversed, or recovered — not even by Anand.
🚫 No family names in source code
Family member names, relationships, and tree structure are stored in a private Google Sheet — not in the app's source code. View Source reveals nothing.
🔒 Account lockout
After 6 wrong password attempts, the account is locked for 30 minutes automatically. This prevents brute-force guessing of passwords.
📧 Email never shared
Your email address is used only for login and password reset. It is never displayed to other family members under any circumstances.
☁️ Google-grade infrastructure
Data is stored in Google Sheets on Anand's private Google account. Photos are stored in Google Drive. Both use Google's enterprise-grade security.
📋 Full audit trail
Every login, registration, and change is logged with a timestamp. Anand can review any unusual activity at any time.
Frequently Asked Questions

Privacy questions, answered honestly

Can someone outside the family see my information?
No. The app requires a valid registered email and password to display anything at all. A person who does not have an account — including someone who knows the URL — sees only a login screen. Family names, the tree, and all contact details are completely hidden from unauthenticated visitors.
Can other family members see my phone number and address?
Yes — but only if you choose to provide them. These fields are entirely optional during registration and can be left blank. If you add them, other registered family members will be able to see them, the same way you would share them on a family WhatsApp group. You can update or remove your details at any time from "Edit My Profile."
Who built this, and who has access to the data?
Anand Sinha built and maintains this application entirely. Data is stored in a private Google Sheet on his personal Google account. No third-party companies, services, or individuals have access to the family data. There are no analytics trackers, advertising networks, or data brokers involved.
Is my password safe?
Yes. Your password is never stored in readable form. It is converted into a one-way cryptographic hash (SHA-256 with a unique salt) before being saved. This means that even if the Google Sheet were ever accessed by an unauthorised party, your actual password could not be determined from it. Anand cannot read your password either — if you forget it, he can only reset it, not tell you what it was.
Can I remove myself from the directory?
Yes. Contact Anand on WhatsApp (+1 248 872 3057) and he will remove your account and all associated data from the system. Your name will still appear on the family tree as an unregistered member (since that is factual family history), but all contact details, email, password, and photo will be permanently deleted.
Will this app ever show ads or sell our data?
Never. This is a personal project built by Anand for the family. There is no business model, no monetisation, and no commercial interest. The only cost is Anand's time.
What happens if I forget my password?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your registered email. A temporary password will be sent to your email within a minute. Use it to log in, then set a new password immediately. The temporary password expires after one hour.
Is this app available on iPhone and Android?
Yes — it is a website, so it works on any phone, tablet, or computer with a browser. On iPhone, open it in Safari, tap the Share button, and select "Add to Home Screen" to create an icon that opens it like an app. On Android, tap the three-dot menu in Chrome and select "Add to Home screen."
User Guide

How to use the app

Once you are signed in, you will see two main sections — the Family Tree and the Directory.

🌳 Family Tree
→
Pan and zoom
Drag to move around the tree. Pinch to zoom on mobile. Use the + / − buttons or scroll on desktop. The Reset button re-centres the view.
→
Expand a branch
Tap the small ＋ button below any node to reveal their children and grandchildren. Tap − to collapse the branch again.
→
View a person's details
Tap any circle on the tree to open a card with their birthday, anniversary, address, and WhatsApp button. Tap the background or ✕ to close.
→
Download
Use the PDF button to save a printable family tree. Use PNG for an image you can share on WhatsApp. Use Excel to download the full directory as a spreadsheet.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Directory
→
Browse by family branch
Members are grouped by the Gen 1 family branch — for example "Jaya Srivastava's Family." Tap a branch header to expand or collapse it.
→
Search
Type any name, city, or phone number in the search bar to instantly filter the directory across all branches.
→
Tap a registered member
Members who have joined show a ✅ badge. Tap their row to see full contact details and open WhatsApp directly.
✏️ Editing Your Profile
→
Open your profile
Tap your name in the top-right corner of the screen. A dropdown appears — select "Edit My Profile."
→
Update your details
Change your phone number, address, photo, birthday, or anniversary. All changes are saved immediately to the directory.
→
Adding a photo
Tap the photo circle and select an image from your phone. Photos are resized automatically — any clear headshot works well.
