WHY A4H ENDORSES

Rakhi Israni

for U.S. House of Representatives (CA-14)

A4H is proud to support Rakhi Israni for Congress in California's 14th District. As an attorney, educator, entrepreneur, and community leader, Rakhi brings a practical, solutions-oriented approach focused on affordability, education, public safety, and community engagement.

🎓

Education, Merit & Upward Mobility

Rakhi's background as an educator and entrepreneur reflects the values of hard work, academic excellence, and upward mobility that resonate strongly with many Indian American and Hindu American families.

🤝

Community-Centered Leadership

She has demonstrated deep involvement in local community organizations, nonprofit initiatives, and civic engagement efforts across the Bay Area, with a focus on practical outcomes over political theater.

💼

Economic Opportunity & Affordability

Rakhi has emphasized tackling rising costs, supporting small businesses, and strengthening economic opportunity for working families and professionals in California.

🕉️

Respectful Engagement with Hindu & Indian American Communities

A4H values leaders who engage respectfully and sincerely with Hindu American and Indian American communities while recognizing their growing contribution to civic and economic life.

🛡️

Public Safety & Responsible Governance

She supports balanced, common-sense governance focused on safer neighborhoods, accountability, and restoring public trust in institutions.

🗳️

Representation & Civic Participation

Rakhi's candidacy reflects the growing civic participation of Indian Americans and encourages broader engagement in public service and democratic institutions.

💡

Practical, Solutions-Oriented Approach

A4H appreciates leaders who focus on problem-solving, dialogue, and delivering results rather than ideological division and partisan conflict.
