About the Author
Anand Sinha
Writer · Civic Technologist · Founder, The Signal

Anand Sinha spent decades in corporate life before retiring to focus on the questions that had always mattered most — civilization, identity, history, and the political future of Hindu Americans. The Signal is where he writes them down.

He is actively engaged with Americans for Hindus (A4H), a Political Action Committee working to awaken Hindu Americans to the urgency of civic participation. He conceived, researched, and built entirely on his own the A4H Elections Hub 2026 — a fifty-state voter guide and candidate tracking system. He is based in Troy, Michigan.
