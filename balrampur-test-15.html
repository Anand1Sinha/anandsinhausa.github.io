How to Register

Step 1 — Family Code
Contact Anand Sinha on WhatsApp (+1 248 872 3057) to get the code. This keeps the directory private to family only.
Step 2 — Your Family
Select which of Nana's children your branch belongs to. Then pick your name from the list. If you are a spouse, select the person you are married to and choose "Spouse" as your relationship.
Step 3 — Your Details
Your name, phone, city, birthday and anniversary. All fields except your first name are optional. Adding a photo means it will appear in the family tree — highly recommended!
Step 4 — Your Account
Choose an email and a password (at least 8 characters, one number). Tick "Keep me signed in" on the login screen so you never need to enter it again.
