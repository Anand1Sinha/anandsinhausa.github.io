WHY A4H ENDORSES

Ashok Baddi

for Michigan House of Representatives (HD-056)

A4H is proud to support Ashok Baddi for the Michigan House of Representatives. A businessman, community advocate, and engaged civic leader, Ashok represents the values of entrepreneurship, family, opportunity, and community participation that resonate strongly with Hindu American families across Michigan.

💼

Entrepreneurship & Economic Opportunity

Ashok understands the challenges facing small businesses, working professionals, and immigrant families. He supports policies that encourage economic growth, entrepreneurship, and greater opportunity for local communities.

🤝

Community Engagement & Civic Participation

Ashok has demonstrated active involvement in community and civic initiatives while encouraging broader participation by Hindu American communities in public life.

🎓

Education, Merit & Upward Mobility

A4H values leaders who reflect the principles of education, merit, hard work, and upward mobility — values that remain central to many Hindu American families.

🏡

Support for Families & Local Communities

Ashok's focus on practical governance and community-centered leadership reflects the concerns of working families seeking safer neighborhoods, stronger schools, and economic stability.

🕉️

Respectful Engagement with Hindu American Communities

A4H appreciates leaders who engage sincerely and respectfully with Hindu American communities while recognizing their growing civic and economic contributions.

💡

Practical, Solutions-Oriented Leadership

Ashok brings a grounded, practical approach focused on dialogue, problem-solving, and constructive engagement rather than ideological division.

🗳️

Representation & Grassroots Leadership

His candidacy reflects the increasing civic engagement of Hindu Americans and encourages broader participation in state and local leadership.
