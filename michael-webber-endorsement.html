WHY A4H ENDORSES

Michael Webber

for Michigan State Senate (District 9)

A4H is proud to endorse Senator Michael Webber for re-election to Michigan's 9th Senate District. Senator Webber has consistently engaged with Michigan's Hindu American community and has demonstrated a willingness to recognize and celebrate the traditions that enrich our state. His leadership extends beyond legislation to building meaningful relationships with diverse communities across Metro Detroit, including introducing a Michigan Senate resolution recognizing the significance of Diwali.

🛐

Recognizing Hindu Heritage

Senator Webber introduced and led the Michigan Senate's recognition of Diwali, acknowledging its historic, cultural and religious significance while celebrating the values of hope, light and community shared by many Michigan families.

🤝

Accessible and Engaged Leadership

Representing Troy, Rochester, Rochester Hills, Utica and neighboring communities, Senator Webber has built a reputation for being approachable, responsive and engaged with constituents from diverse backgrounds.

🏛️

Experienced Public Service

Having served in both the Michigan House of Representatives and the Michigan Senate, Senator Webber brings legislative experience and an understanding of state government that benefits his constituents.

💼

Supporting Economic Opportunity

A4H values leaders who encourage economic growth, entrepreneurship, fiscal responsibility and opportunities for businesses and working families to prosper.

🌏

Respect for Diverse Communities

Senator Webber has demonstrated respect for Michigan's growing Hindu American and Indian American communities by engaging with community organizations and recognizing their contributions to the state's economic, educational and civic life.

⚖️

Practical, Solutions-Oriented Leadership

A4H appreciates elected officials who focus on practical governance, constructive dialogue and constituent service while working across communities to address local issues.

🤝

Building Bridges Through Civic Engagement

A4H believes that lasting public policy is built through dialogue, mutual respect, and active civic participation. Senator Webber has demonstrated a commitment to engaging with Michigan's diverse communities, including Hindu Americans, recognizing that every community deserves a voice in shaping Michigan's future. His willingness to build relationships across communities reflects the collaborative leadership that A4H values and supports.
