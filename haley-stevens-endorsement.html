Why A4H Endorses

Haley Stevens

for U.S. Senator of Michigan

A4H is proud to endorse Haley Stevens for U.S. Senator of Michigan. We believe she brings the proven leadership, manufacturing expertise, and unwavering commitment to Michigan families needed to represent the Great Lakes State effectively in Washington.

🏭

Champion of Manufacturing & Innovation

Haley Stevens has deep expertise in manufacturing policy, having served as Chief of Staff for President Obama's Auto Task Force that helped rescue Michigan's auto industry. As Chair of the Research and Technology Subcommittee, she championed the CHIPS and Science Act, bringing billions in investments to American manufacturing and protecting supply chains. Her "Manufacturing Mondays" program demonstrates consistent engagement with Michigan businesses and workers.

💼

Economic Opportunity & Job Creation

Stevens has focused her career on strengthening Michigan's economy through workforce development, advanced manufacturing, and technology investment. She worked with Republicans and Democrats to pass the USMCA trade agreement and secure fair trade policies for Michigan workers. Her leadership on STEM education and workforce training programs aligns with A4H's values of merit, education, and creating pathways to opportunity for all communities.

🎓

Education & STEM Excellence

As a member of the Women in STEM Caucus and champion of technology education, Stevens has consistently advocated for expanding opportunities in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics fields. Her background in launching America's first online training program in manufacturing and design technology demonstrates commitment to innovation in education and workforce development.

🤝

Bipartisan Problem-Solving

Stevens has demonstrated a willingness to work across party lines to deliver results for Michigan. She worked with Republicans to pass critical manufacturing legislation, secured bipartisan support for infrastructure investments, and has focused on practical solutions over partisan gridlock. A4H values leaders who can build coalitions and get things done for their constituents.

🏡

Michigan Roots & Commitment

Born and raised in Rochester Hills and Birmingham, Michigan, Stevens is a homegrown leader who has dedicated her career to serving Michigan families. Unlike opponents who left the state, Stevens has remained committed to Michigan communities. She understands the challenges facing suburban and urban families, small businesses, and working parents across the state.

👥

Grassroots Campaign & Community Support

Stevens has built a grassroots campaign with 95% of donations under $200 from nurses, factory workers, and everyday Michiganders. She has earned endorsements from the Michigan Democratic Party Black Caucus, former Speaker Joe Tate, and mayors across Michigan. A4H appreciates leaders who remain accessible and accountable to the diverse communities they serve.

🛡️

Protecting Earned Benefits & Fighting for Families

Stevens has been a vocal defender of Social Security, Medicare, and the earned benefits that Michigan families depend on. She has stood up against efforts to cut these critical programs and has fought to lower costs for working families. A4H values leaders who protect the economic security of all communities, including immigrant families and seniors.

🕉️

Alignment with A4H Values & Community Engagement

Based on our ongoing engagement with Congresswoman Stevens and her office, A4H appreciates her openness to dialogue on issues affecting Hindu American and Indian American families in Michigan. She has demonstrated respect for religious diversity and an understanding of the contributions of immigrant communities to Michigan's economy and culture. Stevens has shown willingness to listen to concerns about religious bias, hate crimes targeting temples and community centers, and ensuring equal dignity for all faith communities. A4H values leaders who make themselves accessible to diverse communities and who approach constituent concerns with sincerity and thoughtfulness.
