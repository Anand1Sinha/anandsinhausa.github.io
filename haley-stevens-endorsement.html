WHY A4H ENDORSES

Haley Stevens

for U.S. Senator of Michigan

A4H is proud to endorse Haley Stevens for U.S. Senator of Michigan. We believe she brings the proven leadership, manufacturing expertise, and unwavering commitment to Michigan families needed to represent the Great Lakes State effectively in Washington.

🏭

Manufacturing & Economic Leadership

Proven track record in auto manufacturing and advanced manufacturing sectors. Understands Michigan's economy and is committed to bringing good-paying jobs back to the Great Lakes State. Champions policies that support American manufacturing and innovation.

💼

Proven Congressional Leadership

Has demonstrated effective legislative leadership in the House, delivering results for Michigan families. Known for bipartisan problem-solving and practical approach to governance. Strong record of constituent service and community engagement.

🎓

Education & Opportunity

Committed to expanding educational opportunities, workforce development, and pathways to the middle class. Supports policies that help families achieve economic security and upward mobility through education and skills training.

🚗

Champion of Innovation & Technology

Strong advocate for electric vehicles, clean energy, and emerging technologies. Understands the importance of positioning Michigan at the forefront of the innovation economy while protecting existing manufacturing jobs.

👥

Inclusive Community Engagement

Has demonstrated consistent engagement with diverse communities including Indian American and Hindu American organizations. Values the contributions of immigrant communities to Michigan's economic and cultural fabric.

🤝

Bipartisan Collaboration

Known for working across the aisle to deliver practical results. Focuses on finding common ground and building coalitions to advance Michigan's interests. Prioritizes problem-solving over partisan politics.

🕉️

Engagement with Hindu & Indian American Community

Has maintained ongoing engagement with Hindu American organizations and shown openness to dialogue on issues important to the Indian American community, including religious diversity, addressing bias and hate crimes, and supporting policies that benefit immigrant families.

A4H is a 501(c)(4) nonprofit organization. Our endorsements are based on careful evaluation of candidates' positions and alignment with our values.
