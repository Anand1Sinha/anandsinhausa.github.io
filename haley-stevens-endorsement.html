WHY A4H ENDORSES

Haley Stevens

for U.S. Senator of Michigan

A4H is proud to endorse Haley Stevens for U.S. Senator of Michigan. We believe she brings the proven leadership, manufacturing expertise, and unwavering commitment to Michigan families needed to represent the Great Lakes State effectively in Washington.

🏭

Champion of Manufacturing & Innovation

Chief of Staff for President Obama's Auto Task Force that rescued Michigan's auto industry. Championed the CHIPS and Science Act, bringing billions in manufacturing investments and protecting supply chains.

💼

Economic Opportunity & Job Creation

Focused on workforce development, advanced manufacturing, and technology investment. Worked across party lines to pass USMCA and secure fair trade policies for Michigan workers.

🎓

Education & STEM Excellence

Member of Women in STEM Caucus and champion of technology education. Launched America's first online training program in manufacturing and design technology.

🤝

Bipartisan Problem-Solving

Works across party lines to deliver results. Secured bipartisan support for infrastructure investments and focuses on practical solutions over partisan gridlock.

🏡

Michigan Roots & Commitment

Born and raised in Rochester Hills and Birmingham, Michigan. A homegrown leader who has dedicated her career to serving Michigan families and understands the challenges they face.

👥

Grassroots Campaign & Community Support

Built a campaign with 95% of donations under $200 from nurses, factory workers, and everyday Michiganders. Endorsed by Michigan Democratic Party Black Caucus and mayors across Michigan.

🛡️

Protecting Earned Benefits & Fighting for Families

Vocal defender of Social Security, Medicare, and earned benefits. Fights to lower costs for working families and protects economic security for all communities.

🕉️

Alignment with A4H Values & Community Engagement

Based on our ongoing engagement, demonstrates openness to dialogue on issues affecting Hindu American and Indian American families. Shows willingness to listen to concerns about religious bias, hate crimes, and ensuring equal dignity for all faith communities.
