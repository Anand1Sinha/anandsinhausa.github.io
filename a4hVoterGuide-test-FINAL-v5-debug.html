About This Voter Guide

The Americans4Hindus Voter Guide helps Hindu American voters make informed decisions in the 2026 elections. We provide comprehensive candidate information across federal, state, and local races, along with A4H endorsements for candidates who align with our community's values.

How to Use This Tool:

  • A4H Endorsed: View all candidates endorsed by Americans4Hindus across the country
  • My State: Browse all candidates running for office in your state
  • My ZIP Code: Find the specific candidates who will appear on your ballot

Understanding Candidate Ratings:

  • A4H Endorsed: Candidates who align with Americans4Hindus values and have demonstrated support for our community's interests
  • A4H Concern: Candidates whose positions or actions are not aligned with our values and may work against the interests of our community
