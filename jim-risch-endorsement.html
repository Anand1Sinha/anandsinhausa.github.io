WHY A4H ENDORSES

Jim Risch

for U.S. Senate (Idaho)

A4H is proud to support Jim Risch for U.S. Senate. With decades of public service experience and a reputation for principled leadership, Jim has consistently championed national security, fiscal responsibility, constitutional freedoms, and effective governance. His steady leadership and commitment to American strength and stability reflect values important to many Hindu American and Indian American families.

🛡️

National Security & Global Leadership

As a senior leader on foreign policy and national security issues, Jim has consistently supported a strong America that can deter threats, protect allies, and maintain global stability.

💰

Fiscal Responsibility & Economic Stability

Jim has advocated for responsible government spending, economic growth, lower taxes, and policies that support long-term financial stability for American families and businesses.

📜

Constitutional Rights & Religious Freedom

A4H values leaders who support constitutional protections, individual liberty, and the freedom of all Americans to openly practice their faith and traditions.

⚡

Energy, Innovation & Economic Opportunity

Jim supports policies that encourage innovation, entrepreneurship, domestic energy production, and job creation that strengthen American competitiveness and economic resilience.

🎖️

Experience, Stability & Public Service

With decades of leadership experience at both the state and federal levels, Jim brings institutional knowledge, steadiness, and a practical governing approach focused on long-term results.

🤝

Respect for Communities & Civic Engagement

A4H appreciates leaders who engage respectfully with diverse communities and recognize the growing contributions of Hindu Americans and Indian Americans to the nation's civic and economic life.

🏔️

Idaho Values & Common-Sense Leadership

Jim has built a reputation for practical, common-sense leadership rooted in accountability, service, and a commitment to representing the interests of the people he serves.
