WHY A4H ENDORSES

Sam Liccardo

for U.S. House of Representatives (CA-16)

A4H is proud to support Sam Liccardo for Congress in California's 16th District. As an experienced public servant, former mayor, and pragmatic policy leader, Sam brings a solutions-oriented approach focused on innovation, economic opportunity, public safety, and effective governance for one of America's most dynamic regions.

💡

Innovation, Technology & Economic Growth

Representing the heart of Silicon Valley, Sam understands the importance of technology, entrepreneurship, innovation, and maintaining America's global economic competitiveness in a rapidly evolving economy.

⚖️

Experienced Public Service Leadership

Sam's years of executive and public policy experience reflect a strong commitment to governance, civic leadership, and addressing complex urban and economic challenges through practical solutions.

🛡️

Public Safety & Community Stability

A4H values leaders who prioritize safe communities, accountable governance, and policies that improve quality of life for families, professionals, and local neighborhoods.

🏡

Housing, Infrastructure & Affordability

Sam has consistently focused on housing affordability, infrastructure modernization, and economic development — issues that deeply impact working families and immigrant communities throughout California.

🕉️

Respectful Engagement with Hindu & Indian American Communities

A4H appreciates leaders who engage sincerely and respectfully with Hindu American and Indian American communities while recognizing their growing contribution to America's technological, economic, and civic success.

🤝

Pragmatic, Solutions-Oriented Governance

A4H supports leaders who focus on constructive dialogue, data-driven policymaking, and practical problem-solving rather than ideological polarization and partisan conflict.

🎓

Opportunity, Education & Upward Mobility

Sam's leadership reflects support for innovation, educational opportunity, economic mobility, and the broader American aspiration of creating opportunity through hard work and civic participation.
