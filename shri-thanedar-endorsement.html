WHY A4H ENDORSES

Shri Thanedar

for U.S. House of Representatives (MI-13)

A4H is proud to support Shri Thanedar for Congress in Michigan's 13th District. An entrepreneur, scientist, philanthropist, and immigrant success story, Shri represents resilience, opportunity, innovation, and civic participation — values that resonate deeply with many Hindu American families.

💼

Entrepreneurship & Economic Opportunity

Shri's journey from immigrant student to successful entrepreneur reflects the spirit of hard work, risk-taking, innovation, and economic mobility that defines the American dream for many immigrant communities.

🔬

STEM, Innovation & Education

With a background in science and business leadership, Shri strongly represents the values of education, merit, entrepreneurship, and innovation that remain central to many Hindu American households.

🏛️

Founder of the Hindu, Buddhist, Sikh, and Jain American Congressional Caucus

Rep. Thanedar founded this bipartisan caucus in Congress, giving our community a formal, ongoing seat at the table on issues like temple security, religious discrimination, and civil rights.

📜

Author of H.Res. 1131

Rep. Thanedar authored H.Res. 1131, the first congressional resolution condemning Hinduphobia and attacks on Hindu places of worship — putting anti-Hindu bigotry into the official congressional record.

🗳️

Representation & Civic Participation

As one of the few Hindu American voices serving in Congress, Shri's leadership encourages broader civic engagement and political participation within Hindu American communities.

🤝

Commitment to Public Service

Shri has demonstrated long-standing engagement in philanthropy, community initiatives, and public service while advocating for economic development, healthcare access, and opportunity for working families.

🕉️

Respectful Engagement with Hindu American Communities

A4H values leaders who engage sincerely and respectfully with Hindu American communities while recognizing their growing contribution to America's civic, economic, and cultural landscape.

🇺🇸

Immigrant Success & Upward Mobility

Shri's personal story reflects perseverance, educational achievement, and upward mobility — inspiring many first-generation immigrant families who value opportunity through hard work and determination.

💡

Solutions-Oriented Leadership

A4H appreciates leaders who focus on practical governance, dialogue, and constructive problem-solving rather than division and ideological extremism.
