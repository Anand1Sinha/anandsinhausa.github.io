Every fall, Stonehaven Woods East comes alive with the sounds of neighbors catching up, kids running through the backyard, and the aroma of food being shared across 42 households that have made this corner of Troy something truly special.

This year's Annual Social is a chance to slow down, step outside, and reconnect with the people who make this neighborhood home. Whether you've lived here for twenty years or just moved in, you belong here — and we can't wait to see you.

What to Expect
  • 🍕
    Food & Refreshments
    Apple cider, coffee, donuts, samosas, and pizza — plus whatever our neighbors bring to share. It's always better together.
  • 🤝
    Neighborly Conversation
    Two hours of unhurried time with the people next door, down the street, and around the corner. The kind of afternoon you'll look forward to all year.
  • 👶
    Kids Welcome
    Bring the whole family. Little ones and older kids alike — the backyard is big and the neighborhood is safe. Let them run.
  • 🎯
    Bring Your Competitive Spirit!
    Dust off your bocce ball, cornhole set, frisbee, or any lawn game you love. We're making this a real neighborhood showdown — bragging rights included. Coordinated by your Social Committee.
  • 🍂
    A Beautiful October Afternoon
    October 4th in Troy. The leaves will be turning, the air will be crisp, and the backyard at 3887 Fadi Drive will be ready to welcome you.

This is more than a neighborhood get-together. It's a reminder that Stonehaven Woods East isn't just a place to live — it's a community that looks out for each other, celebrates together, and makes Troy a little warmer every year.
