WHY A4H ENDORSES

Mike Lawler

for U.S. House of Representatives (NY-17)

A4H is proud to support Mike Lawler for Congress in New York's 17th District. Mike has earned a reputation as a pragmatic, bipartisan leader focused on affordability, public safety, economic opportunity, and effective governance. His commitment to practical problem-solving and constituent service reflects values that resonate strongly with many Hindu American and Indian American families.

🤝

Bipartisan & Solutions-Oriented Leadership

Mike has demonstrated a willingness to work across party lines to advance practical solutions on issues affecting families, businesses, and local communities. A4H values leaders who prioritize results over partisan division.

💼

Economic Opportunity & Affordability

Mike has consistently focused on addressing cost-of-living concerns, supporting small businesses, and promoting economic policies that help working families achieve financial stability and upward mobility.

🎓

Education, Merit & Upward Mobility

A4H appreciates leaders who support educational excellence, workforce development, and opportunities for individuals to succeed through hard work, talent, and achievement.

🛡️

Public Safety & Community Security

Mike has advocated for safe communities, strong local institutions, and policies that protect families while maintaining public confidence in government and law enforcement.

🕉️

Respectful Engagement with Hindu & Indian American Communities

A4H values leaders who engage sincerely and respectfully with Hindu American and Indian American communities and recognize their growing contributions to America's economic, civic, educational, and cultural success.

🗳️

Commitment to Civic Responsibility

Mike's record reflects a strong commitment to public service, constituent engagement, accountability, and strengthening democratic institutions through responsible governance.

💡

Practical Leadership for the Future

A4H supports leaders who focus on long-term solutions, economic competitiveness, infrastructure, innovation, and ensuring that future generations have greater opportunities to succeed.
