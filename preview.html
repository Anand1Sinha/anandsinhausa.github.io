Introduction to Mahjong

Objective: Mahjong is a tile game where each player draws and discards tiles to complete a winning hand. A normal winning hand usually has 4 sets and 1 pair.

Simple winning structure

Set 1Set 2Set 3Set 4Pair

A set can be a sequence, three matching tiles, or four matching tiles. A pair is two identical tiles.

How the game is played

  • Each player starts with 13 tiles.
  • On your turn, you draw one tile and discard one tile.
  • You try to arrange your tiles into legal sets and a pair.
  • When your hand is complete, you call Mahjong.

Windy Chow is a named winning pattern built around the four winds and one chow in each suit.

What you need

  • One East, one South, one West, and one North wind.
  • One chow in Bamboo.
  • One chow in Dots.
  • One chow in Characters.
  • One pair, usually a wind tile depending on the house rules.
