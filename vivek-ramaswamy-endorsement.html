WHY A4H ENDORSES

Vivek Ramaswamy

for Governor of Ohio

A4H is proud to endorse Vivek Ramaswamy for Governor of Ohio. We believe he brings exceptional leadership, entrepreneurial vision, and a commitment to making Ohio the most affordable, opportunity-rich state in America.

💼

Entrepreneurial Success & Economic Vision

Proven biotech entrepreneur who founded Roivant Sciences and built successful businesses. Brings private sector experience and innovation mindset to governance. Committed to creating jobs and making Ohio the most affordable state for working families.

💰

Tax Relief & Affordability

Proposes eliminating state income tax and lowering property taxes to reverse the exodus of young people from Ohio. Focused on making Ohio affordable for families and competitive for businesses.

🎓

Education Excellence & Academic Standards

Committed to improving K-12 performance and raising academic standards at public schools. Harvard and Yale educated, brings strong emphasis on merit, excellence, and educational achievement—values that resonate with many Indian American families.

⚡

Energy Independence & Economic Growth

Plans to increase energy production by promoting fracking and new nuclear plants. Understands that abundant, affordable energy is essential for economic prosperity and job creation.

🇮🇳

Representation of Indian American Community

Son of legal immigrants from Kerala, India, who came to America in the 1970s. Born and raised in Cincinnati, represents the success story of Indian American families. Brings lived experience of the immigrant journey and commitment to the American Dream.

🕉️

Hindu American Leadership & Faith

A practicing Hindu, married to Dr. Apoorva Tewari, an otolaryngology specialist and professor at Ohio State University. Father of three children: Karthik, Arjun, and Savithri. Represents Hindu American values of family, education, hard work, and service.

📚

Intellectual Leadership & Clear Vision

Author of multiple books including "Woke, Inc." Articulate advocate for merit-based policies and opposition to identity politics. Brings intellectual rigor and principled leadership to addressing Ohio's challenges.

🤝

Maturity & Inclusive Approach

Has demonstrated growth in his approach to governance, calling out bigotry and racism while adopting a congenial, inclusive tone. Focuses on practical solutions that benefit all Ohioans regardless of background.
