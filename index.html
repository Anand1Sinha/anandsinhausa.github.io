Anand Sinha

Anand Sinha is a technology executive, strategist, writer, and community leader who writes about the forces shaping our world — from artificial intelligence and geopolitics to economics, elections, and Indian civilization. Following more than four decades in global technology and consulting leadership, he now focuses on independent research, public affairs, digital strategy, and community initiatives, serving in leadership roles across A4H, UPAD, and his local community. He lives in Metro Detroit, Michigan.
