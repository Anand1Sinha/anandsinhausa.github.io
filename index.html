About Anand Sinha
Anand Sinha

Technology. Public policy. Economics. Indian civilization. Independent analysis grounded in evidence, history, and long-term thinking.

Anand Sinha is a technology executive, strategist, writer, and community leader who writes about the forces shaping our world — from artificial intelligence and geopolitics to economics, elections, and Indian civilization. His work emphasizes evidence over ideology, historical context over headlines, and long-term trends over daily news cycles.

Following more than four decades in global technology and consulting leadership, he now focuses on independent research, public affairs, and community initiatives from Metro Detroit, Michigan.
