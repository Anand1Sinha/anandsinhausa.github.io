WHY A4H ENDORSES

Jay Vaingankar

for U.S. House of Representatives (NJ-12)

A4H is proud to support Jay Vaingankar for Congress in New Jersey's 12th District. A former White House and Department of Energy official, Jay represents a new generation of leadership focused on economic opportunity, innovation, civic engagement, and public service.

🚀

Next-Generation Leadership

Jay brings energy, professionalism, and a forward-looking perspective shaped by public service experience at the White House and Department of Energy.

💡

Education, Innovation & Economic Growth

His focus on innovation, entrepreneurship, and clean energy aligns with A4H's support for policies that strengthen economic opportunity and long-term competitiveness.

🇺🇸

Immigrant Success Story & American Opportunity

As the son of immigrants from Mumbai, Jay's story reflects the American Dream and the contributions immigrant communities make to the nation's economic and civic success.

🗳️

Civic Engagement & Community Outreach

A4H values candidates who actively engage diverse communities and encourage greater civic participation among younger generations and immigrant families.

🎓

Merit, Professional Excellence & Public Service

Jay's academic and policy background reflects a commitment to merit, public service, and thoughtful leadership grounded in real-world experience.

🤝

Inclusive & Respectful Leadership

He has demonstrated a willingness to engage constructively across communities and backgrounds while promoting unity and civic dialogue.

🇮🇳

Expanding Indian American Representation

Jay's candidacy reflects the growing participation of Indian Americans in national leadership and inspires broader political engagement within the community.
