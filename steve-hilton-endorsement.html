WHY A4H ENDORSES

Steve Hilton

for Governor of California

A4H is proud to endorse Steve Hilton for Governor of California. We believe he brings the leadership, vision, and commitment needed to build a stronger, safer, and more prosperous California for all.

📊

Economic Opportunity & Affordability

Focused on lowering the cost of living, supporting small businesses, and expanding opportunity for working families across California. Champions "Califordable" agenda: $3 gas, cutting electric bills in half, and making first $100,000 tax-free.

🎓

Merit, Education & Innovation

Strong emphasis on educational excellence, entrepreneurship, innovation, and upward mobility—values that resonate deeply with many Indian American families. Committed to ensuring 100% of students meet state math and English standards.

🛡️

Public Safety & Accountability

Advocates practical, solutions-oriented governance with a focus on safer communities and restoring confidence in public institutions. Committed to enforcing the law and supporting law enforcement officers.

👥

Inclusive Community Engagement

Has demonstrated a willingness to engage respectfully with Indian American and Hindu American organizations. As the son of Hungarian refugees who fled communism, brings personal understanding of the immigrant experience and American opportunity.

🤝

Respect for Religious & Cultural Diversity

Supports an environment where all communities are treated with dignity, fairness, and equal respect under the law. Committed to protecting religious freedom for all faith communities.

⭐

Recognition of Immigrant Contributions

Appreciates the vital role immigrant, professional, and entrepreneurial communities play in California's economic and civic success. Understands that legal immigration and entrepreneurship are essential to California's prosperity.

👤

Accessible & Solutions-Oriented Leadership

A4H values leaders who are thoughtful, approachable, and willing to engage constructively across communities and viewpoints. Hilton has demonstrated commitment to practical problem-solving over partisan gridlock.
