Anand Sinha

Anand Sinha is a technology executive, strategist, writer, and community leader who writes about the forces shaping our world — from artificial intelligence and geopolitics to economics, elections, and Indian civilization. His work emphasizes evidence over ideology, historical context over headlines, and long-term trends over daily news cycles.

Following more than four decades in global technology and consulting leadership, Anand now focuses on independent research, public affairs, digital strategy, and community initiatives. He serves as Chief Information Officer and Chair of Social Media for Americans for Hindus Advocacy (A4H), Chairperson of the UP Association of Detroit (UPAD), and President of the Stonehaven Woods Homeowners Association.

He previously served as President of the Indus Center for Academic Excellence (ICAE), a Michigan nonprofit dedicated to STEM education and academic excellence, and as President of UPAD.

He lives in Metro Detroit, Michigan.
