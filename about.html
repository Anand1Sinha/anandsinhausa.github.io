Biography

Anand Sinha is a technology executive, strategist, writer, and community leader who writes about the forces shaping our world — from artificial intelligence and geopolitics to economics, elections, and Indian civilization. His work emphasizes evidence over ideology, historical context over headlines, and long-term trends over daily news cycles.

Following more than four decades in global technology and consulting leadership — spanning roles at Ciber Global, Tech Mahindra, Cognizant, and NTT DATA — Anand now focuses on independent research, public affairs, digital strategy, and community initiatives. He serves in leadership roles across A4H, UPAD, and his local community in Troy, Michigan.

"Be at the table — or be on the menu."

As CIO and Chair of Social Media for Americans for Hindus Advocacy (A4H), he works to awaken Hindu Americans to the urgency of civic participation and political representation. He conceived and built entirely on his own the A4H Elections Hub 2026 — a fifty-state voter guide and candidate tracking system designed to give Hindu Americans a seat at the table in American political life.

As Chairperson of the UP Association of Detroit (UPAD), he oversees the community's cultural programming, civic outreach, and the UPAD Times — a digital magazine covering culture, food, history, and diaspora life from Uttar Pradesh and Metro Detroit.
